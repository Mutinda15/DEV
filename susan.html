Susan Yiapan

Susan Yiapan is the Co-founder of Multi Seed, a business that is based in Olorropil Ward, Narok County, Kenya

Susan Yiapan identified the business opportunity because of serious shortage of Certified Potato Seed in Kenya

Multi Seed was registered in July 2022 and specializes in producing Certified Shangi Potato Seed as a Seed Merchant

By mid 2024, Multi Seed serves 300 farmers with a potential to reach over 2,000 farmers

Multi Seed has 2 stores with a total capacity of 200,000 kgs of Potatoes, and located 20km apart in close proximity to the local farmers in Olorropil and Olokurto Wards.

Challenges

Susan faces challenges with storage space, mechanization, transport and availability of elite Potato Seed material

Vision

To be a leading, reputable, producer and supplier of good Quality Certified Shangi potato seed and to achieve a strong brand reputation

Mission

To collaborate with reputable potato breeders, regulators and stakeholders to consistently produce and avail highest quality Shangi potato seed that meets customers needs at the right time
